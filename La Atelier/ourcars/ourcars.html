Available Cars

The Porsche 911 is an iconic sports car that has been in productive since 1963.It feautres a rear-engine, rear-wheel, distinctive design. The 911 is available inn various models,including the Camera, turbp, GT3 and Targo, each offering different levels of perfomance and customization options.

McLaren 720S: The McLaren 720S is a mid-engine supercar known for its exceptional performance, advanced technology, and striking design. It features a lightweight carbon fiber chassis and body construction, with a twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing up to 710 horsepower. The 720S offers blistering acceleration, precise handling, and a luxurious interior with advanced infotainment systems.

The Maserati GranTurismo features a timeless and elegant design, with flowing lines, muscular curves, and a long hood that houses the powerful engine. It has a classic GT silhouette, with a low-slung profile, elongated front grille, and distinctive Trident logo on the front grille and rear pillar. The GranTurismo's design is inspired by Maserati's rich heritage of producing high-performance GT cars, combining aerodynamic efficiency with Italian flair

The Rolls-Royce Phantom is powered by a potent twin-turbocharged 6.75-liter V12 engine, which delivers smooth and effortless performance. The engine produces ample power and torque, providing seamless acceleration and refined cruising capabilities. The Phantom offers a serene and comfortable driving experience, with advanced suspension systems that deliver a smooth ride and precise handling.

The Jaguar XJ220 features a sleek and aerodynamic design, with a low-slung profile, aggressive front fascia, and distinctive rear spoiler. It has a lightweight aluminum chassis and composite body panels, contributing to its exceptional performance and handling. The XJ220's design was heavily influenced by its racing heritage, with input from Jaguar's motorsport division

Bugatti Chiron: The Bugatti Chiron is a hypercar that represents the pinnacle of automotive engineering and luxury. It features a distinctive design, with aerodynamic styling and advanced materials such as carbon fiber. The Chiron is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine, producing up to 1,500 horsepower and achieving a top speed of over 260 mph. It offers unparalleled performance, comfort, and exclusivity

The BMW M8 Gran Coupe features a sleek and aggressive design, with a low-slung profile, sculpted lines, and a distinctive kidney grille at the front. It has a long wheelbase, wide track, and muscular proportions, giving it a commanding presence on the road. The M8 Gran Coupe combines sporty styling cues with luxurious elements, creating a perfect balance between performance and elegance.

Bentley Continental GT: The Bentley Continental GT is a grand tourer known for its luxurious interior, powerful performance, and timeless design. It features a handcrafted cabin with premium materials, advanced technology, and comfortable seating for four passengers. The Continental GT is available with a range of powerful engines, including a twin-turbocharged W12 engine, delivering exceptional performance and refinement.

The Aston Martin DB11 features a sleek and aerodynamic design, with elegant lines and a distinctive front grille inspired by Aston Martin's design language. It has a muscular stance and a low-slung profile, giving it a sporty and aggressive appearance. The DB11's design combines classic Aston Martin styling cues with modern elements, creating a timeless and sophisticated look.
